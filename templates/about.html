About Uniglobe Lifestyles

Uniglobe Lifestyles is a global sourcing studio headquartered in India with operational reach in Foshan, China — the heart of the world’s furniture and manufacturing ecosystem.

We work closely with architects, developers, hospitality brands, and private clients to source luxury furniture, lighting, decor, and materials directly from trusted Chinese manufacturers.

Our role goes beyond sourcing. We manage factory matching, customization, quality control, and international logistics — ensuring seamless delivery from China to your project site.
